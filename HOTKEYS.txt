Game:
  wasd - movement
  space - stay at place
  j - open/close the door(when you're staying on a button)
    - door can't be closed, if there is a wolf in the cell with door
  r - reload the level

Level-editor:
  wasd - movement
  space or z - stay at place
  j - open the door
  i - return to level initial state(before you start playing it)
